The Foundation

Choiceless Awareness

"The non-dual traditions of the East tell us that real freedom is choiceless awareness. Awareness is our natural state."

Thoughts pull us away. Nudge gently brings us back. Not through force or discipline, but through gentle reminders that what you're looking for is already here.

You don't need a meditation cushion. You don't need silence. You just need to be present — and Nudge helps you find your way back, moment by moment.

Breath Awareness

07

Sound Dissolution

23

Open Sky Gaze
