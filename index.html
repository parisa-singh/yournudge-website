Ancient Wisdom

112 Doorways to
the Present

The Vigyan Bhairav Tantra contains 112 techniques for direct awakening — each one a different path to the same destination: now.

01

Breath Awareness

Rest attention on the gap between breaths — the space where the mind naturally stills.

07

Sound Dissolution

Let any sound arise and dissolve without labeling. Pure listening, without a listener.

23

Open Sky Gaze

Look into the open sky with soft eyes. Let your gaze become as vast as the sky itself.

49

Skin Sensitivity

Feel the air touching your skin. Every sensation becomes a doorway into the present.

77

Pure Witness

Watch thoughts like clouds passing. You are the sky — vast, still, and unchanging.

112

Choiceless Seeing

Look without choosing what to see. Let everything be as it is, without preference.
